    • High Concept: The player solves riddles to escape the evil creature, Gollum
    • Theme: The Hobbit.
    • Mandated Variety: Text Input
    • Prototype Goal: To allow for player to enter text as a means of controlling the game
    • Player Experience Goals: I want players to feel like they are in Bilbo's shoes and having to play a game of riddles with Gollum. I want it to seem like Gollum is coming out of their computer through the speakers.
    • Gameplay: Players use the keyboard to answer the riddles given by the window. Solving the riddles gets the player closer to escape. The next step for the game is to show a meter to show the player how close to the end they are, and could replace the strike markers at the top.
    • Strategies: Using logic to help in solving the riddles, or knowledge of the Hobbit riddle answers.
    • Story/Setting/Premise: This game is based in the World of Middle Earth, which was created by J.R.R. Tolkien. This begins deep under the Misty Mountains where Bilbo has gotten lost and has run into the creature, Gollum, who says that they can play a game of riddles to see if he will eat Bilbo or show him the way out of the maze like tunnels beneath the mountains.
    • Target Audience: Anyone who is a fan of riddles or The Hobbit.
    • Play Time: 5-10 minutes
